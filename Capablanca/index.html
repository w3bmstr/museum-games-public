Capablanca Chess

10x8 board with Archbishop (B+N) and Chancellor (R+N). Capture the king to win.

AI Difficulty

Rules

  • Board is 10x8. New pieces: Archbishop (A) = Bishop+Knight, Chancellor (C) = Rook+Knight.
  • Starting back rank (White): R N B A Q K C B N R. Black mirrors on top.
  • Pawns move/capture like chess pawns; promote to queen on last rank.
  • Goal: capture the enemy king (no check/checkmate enforcement here).
  • No castling or en passant implemented.
  • Controls: mouse click or keyboard arrows + Enter/Space; controller stick/D-pad to move, A to select/move, B to cancel, X reset, LB undo, Y toggles this rules panel, Start/A begins game on start screen.
