Sittuyin

Burmese chess variant. Pawns fixed; pieces start in a preset layout for play. No castling or double-step; promotion to General on last rank.

AI Difficulty

Start

Rules (simplified)

  • Board: 8×8. White at bottom moving up; Black at top moving down. Pawns start on the 3rd rank from each side.
  • Pieces: K king (1 any direction); G general (1 any direction); R chariot (rook); N knight (chess knight); E elephant (forward, forward-diagonals); P pawn (1 forward, capture forward-diagonal).
  • Promotion: pawn promotes to G on the last rank.
  • No castling, no double-step, no en passant.
  • Win by checkmating the enemy king; stalemate counts as a loss for the side with no legal moves.
  • Controls: click squares to select/move; U undo; R reset; H rules.
