Rules

  • Board: 11x11. Center throne and four corners are special squares. Only the king may occupy them.
  • Setup: King and 12 defenders in a diamond around the center. 24 attackers placed in a cross from the edges.
  • Movement: All pieces move any distance orthogonally (like rooks) without jumping. They cannot move onto throne/corners unless king.
  • Capture: Custodial capture. An enemy is captured when sandwiched orthogonally between the mover and a friendly piece or a hostile square (throne/corner). The king is captured when surrounded on all four orthogonal sides by attackers/hostile squares.
  • Win: Defenders win if the king reaches any corner. Attackers win if the king is captured.
  • Controls: mouse/touch click; keyboard arrows + Enter/Space; controller stick/D-pad move, A select/move, B cancel, X reset, LB undo, Y rules, Start opens start screen.
