Rules

  • 10x10 board. Black home row at the top, White at the bottom.
  • Lineup: R N B M Q K C B N R (Marshal = rook+knight, Cardinal = bishop+knight).
  • Pawns start on the 3rd rank (White rank 3, Black rank 8) with empty second ranks. No castling or en passant.
  • Pawns move 1 step, capture diagonally, and promote to a queen on the far rank.
  • Capture the enemy king to win (no check/checkmate detection).
  • Controls: mouse/touch click; keyboard arrows + Enter/Space; controller stick/D-pad move, A select/move, B cancel, X reset, LB undo, Y rules, Start opens start screen.
