Minishogi

5x5 Shogi with drops and promotion. Choose players and start.

AI Difficulty

Start

Rules

  • Board: 5x5. White starts at bottom, Black at top.
  • Pieces: K, G, S, B, R, P. Capture the king to win.
  • Promotion: Pawns/Silvers/Bishops/Rooks auto-promote on entering the enemy zone (top two ranks for White, bottom two for Black). Promoted Pawn/Silver move as Gold; promoted Bishop adds king steps; promoted Rook adds king steps.
  • Drops: Captured pieces flip color, go to your hand, and can be dropped on any empty square (pawns cannot drop on the last rank).
  • Controls: click squares; arrows + Enter/Space; U undo, R reset, H rules.
