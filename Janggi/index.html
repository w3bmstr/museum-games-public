Rules

  • Board: 9x10 with two 3x3 palaces at each side (rows 0-2 and 7-9, cols 3-5). Diagonals inside palace are valid lines.
  • Pieces: R (Chariot/rook), N (Horse), E (Elephant), C (Cannon), G (General), A (Guard), P (Soldier).
  • General and Guards must stay inside their palace. They move 1 step orthogonally; along palace diagonals they may move 1 diagonally.
  • Horse moves 1 orthogonal then 1 diagonal outward; blocked if the first orthogonal square is occupied. Elephant moves 2 orthogonal then 1 diagonal; blocked if any intermediate is occupied.
  • Chariot slides orthogonally. Cannon moves orthogonally; to capture it must jump exactly one piece; for non-capturing move it cannot jump. Cannot jump over empty squares for capture more than one piece.
  • Soldier moves 1 step forward (toward enemy) or sideways; no backward moves. No promotion. Capture by displacement.
  • Win by capturing the enemy General (no check/checkmate detection).
  • Controls: mouse/touch click; keyboard arrows + Enter/Space; controller stick/D-pad move, A select/move, B cancel, X reset, LB undo, Y rules, Start opens start screen.
